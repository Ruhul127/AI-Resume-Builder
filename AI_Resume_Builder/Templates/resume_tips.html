General Resume Tips

Here are some tips to help you create a strong and effective resume:

  • Tailor Your Resume: Customise your resume for each job application by highlighting relevant skills and experiences.
  • Use Action Verbs: Start bullet points with strong action verbs like "managed," "developed," "optimised," etc.
  • Quantify Achievements: Use numbers to showcase your accomplishments (e.g., "Increased sales by 20%").
  • Keep It Concise: Limit your resume to 1-2 pages and avoid unnecessary details.
  • Use a Clean Layout: Ensure your resume is easy to read with clear headings, bullet points, and consistent formatting.
  • Proofread: Check for spelling and grammar errors before submitting your resume.
  • Include Keywords: Use keywords from the job description to pass Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).
  • Highlight Relevant Experience: Focus on experiences that are most relevant to the job you're applying for.
  • Add a Summary or Objective: Include a brief summary or objective at the top of your resume to grab the recruiter's attention.
  • Use a Professional Email Address: Avoid using unprofessional email addresses (e.g., "coolguy123@email.com").
